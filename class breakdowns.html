This area will help those who may not have the greatest grasp of the basic mechanics of the game itself. In this area we will be touching down on the different skills used in Dungeons and Dragons. Below are some basic pieces to help beginners with some things about Dungeons and Dragons.




Stats broken down:

In Dungeons and Dragons there are Six Governing stats that play a very important part in all aspects of the game. When I first learned, I never had much to really go off of except what my Dungeon Master had told me. So I wanted to try and break this down in a way that helped me a lot when I first started. Explaining the stats with tomatoes.

In this game we have six stats: Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma. Each one is going to play an important role in the game as such:

    • Strength:

      Strength governs just how strong your character can squash a tomato.

      Dexterity: Dexterity governs how quickly your character can dodge a tomato being thrown at them
